## 📝 简介
- scAI is an unsupervised approach for integrative analysis of gene expression and chromatin accessibility or DNA methylation proflies measured in the same individual cells. - scAI infers a set of biologically relevant factors, which enable various downstream analyses, including the identification of cell clusters, cluster-specific markers and regulatory relationships. - scAI provides an intuitive way to visualize features (i.e., genes and loci) alongside the cells in two dimensions.
