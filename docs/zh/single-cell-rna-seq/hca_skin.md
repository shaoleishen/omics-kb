## 📝 简介
This repository contains code used for the analysis of the Human Cell Atlas Skin paper published in Science Jan 2021 DOI: 10.1126/science.aba6500 Datasets analysed include: 10X data (Healthy adult Skin, PS skin, AD skin, fetal Skin), IHC for healthy and diseased skin, SS2 data, and healthy skin BCR-/TCR-enriched VDJ data. To quickly browse our data, our online webportal resource may be accessed via (https://developmentcellatlas.ncl.ac.uk/)
