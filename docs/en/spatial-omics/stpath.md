## 📝 Overview
This is our PyTorch implementation for the paper: &gt; Tinglin Huang, Tianyu Liu, Mehrtash Babadi, Rex Ying, and Wengong Jin (2025). STPath: A Generative Foundation Model for Integrating Spatial Transcriptomics and Whole Slide Images. Paper in [bioRxiv](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.04.19.649665v2.abstract). The pretrained model weight is available at [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/tlhuang/STPath).
